The Developers' Guide for Privacy and Security

About PrivacyDev

Inspired by Privacy Guides, PrivacyDev is a platform designed specifically for developers who want to prioritize privacy. We curate privacy best practices and recommendations for tools, frameworks, and libraries with strong privacy protections. Our mission is to provide technically sound insights and guidance to help developers design and build software that respects user privacy.
